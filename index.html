Step 2: Use the Bookmarklet

Once your bookmarklet is saved, follow these steps to mark attendance:

  1. Go to the Faculty Portal and click on My Classes.
  2. Click on Gradebook
  3. Select the class you want to mark attendance for by clicking the course title.
  4. Enter any time in the Time field, and enter any duration in the Length field (e.g., 60 minutes).

  5. Click on the day of the week for which you want to take attendance.
  6. Click your Auto-populate Attendance bookmark to set all blank fields to P (Present).
  7. For students who were Absent (A) or Excused (E), manually change their dropdown selection.
  8. Click Update to save the attendance record.
